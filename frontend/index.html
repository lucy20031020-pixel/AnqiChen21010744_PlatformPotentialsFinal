Inequality addressed

Content removal is powerful, but the reasoning is often hidden.

This add-on helps creators understand why posts may have been removed, what policy area was likely applied, what evidence strengthens an appeal, and how to document unfair or opaque moderation decisions.

Instead of only saying "community guidelines were violated", the system explains what part of the case was flagged, whether the decision appears automated or needs human review, and what appeal routes are available.

Community Guidelines

Users are expected to participate in the platform in a way that does not cause harm to others. The following types of content may be removed, restricted, labelled, or sent for human review.

Hate Speech

Content that attacks, dehumanizes, or encourages discrimination against people based on identity characteristics is not allowed. This includes content targeting race, ethnicity, nationality, religion, gender, sexuality, disability, or other protected identities.

Possible action: removal, warning, account restriction, or human review.

Harassment and Threats

Content that threatens, intimidates, repeatedly targets, humiliates, or encourages others to attack a person or group is not allowed. This includes targeted abuse and coordinated harassment.

Possible action: removal, comment restriction, reporting limits, or escalation to human moderators.

Misinformation and Disinformation

Content that spreads false or misleading information may be restricted or removed, especially when it could cause real-world harm. This includes false information about health, public safety, emergencies, or identity-based rumours.

Possible action: warning label, reduced visibility, fact-check notice, removal, or human review.

Scams, Spam, and Impersonation

Fraudulent content, phishing links, fake giveaways, mass spam, or impersonating other people or organizations is not allowed.

Possible action: content removal, link blocking, account restriction, or platform safety review.

Graphic, Violent, or Exploitative Content

Graphic violence, sexual exploitation, or content that encourages self-harm or real-world violence may be restricted or removed. Some sensitive content may remain visible if it has clear educational, journalistic, artistic, or public-interest context.

Possible action: age restriction, content warning, blur filter, removal, or human review.
